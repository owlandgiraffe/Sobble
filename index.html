Control your Sonos system from your Pebble Watch

Sobble is an application for the Pebble watch, allowing Sonos users to control their Sonos Wireless Speakers from their wrist.

Install it at

Sobble has been developed independently and is in no way affiliated with or sponsored by the company Sonos.

Features

  • Change between each Sonos zone
  • Skip, play, pause, mute and unmute
  • Pause all Sonos zones
  • 'Auto-find' Sonos zones (as long as it's connected subnet 192.168.0.*)
